Seven

One Alarm. One Time. One Choice.

What is Seven?

Seven is not a clock — it's a Ritual.

A minimalist timepiece that only rings once a day — at 7:00 AM.

No other alarms. No snooze. One manual switch to say yes, or to rest instead.

For artists, healers, and anyone rebuilding their mornings, one decision at a time.

Features

  • OLED display
  • Minimalist design
  • Fixed 7:00 AM alarm
  • Matte black rectangular body
  • Physical toggle opt-out switch
  • USB-C powered or rechargeable

The Story Behind Seven

I made Seven because life gets loud. Healing is hard, Discipline is harder and sometimes, the world doesn’t need more options — it needs clarity. Seven is for those choosing structure over chaos, presence over productivity, and silence that means something.

It’s not about alarms. It’s about beginnings.
